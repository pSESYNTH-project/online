pSESYNTH at INQUA 2023

The pSESYNTH Workshop held on July 16th, 2023, was a dynamic gathering focused on exploring the complexities of past socio-environmental systems in the Global South. Organized as part of the INQUA-supported Holocene database project, this event fostered discussions on data synthesis, hypothesis testing frameworks, and collaboration opportunities within the research community.
